About Me

I’m a GIS/RS and Data Analyst with a strong academic background in Geography. My journey into geospatial research began during my undergraduate studies at the University of the Punjab, where I developed an interest in spatial analysis, cartography, and environmental data interpretation.

I started my professional career at WWF-Pakistan, progressing through roles as GIS Intern, GIS Associate, and GIS Officer. Currently, I’m part of the Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS), where I first served as a Research Assistant and now work as a Research Associate, contributing to data-driven projects at the intersection of environment, policy, and urban systems.

My expertise lies in spatial data management, statistical and geospatial analysis, and machine learning applications using ArcGIS, QGIS, Google Earth Engine, Python, R, and PostgreSQL. I’m passionate about automating analytical workflows, developing dashboards, and visualizing complex datasets.
