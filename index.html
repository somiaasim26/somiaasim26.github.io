About Me

I’m a Research Associate focused on Geospatial & Data Analytics — turning satellite imagery and spatial data into clear insights for policy and conservation. My toolkit includes ESRI/ArcGIS, QGIS, Python, R, Google Earth Engine, and Stata. I’ve contributed to projects with WWF-Pakistan and LUMS on urban change, flood analysis, and risk mapping.

Data
